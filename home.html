Natural Habitat of Dogs. The lives of dogs in the wild give powerful clues for the ingredients of a healthy, happy, balanced life for your pet.

Dogs live in many habitats, including prairies, deserts, grasslands, forests, rain forests, coastal regions and arctic zones.

Teddys

Zuchon Teddy Bear Dog. The cross breed between a Bichon Frise and a Shih Tzu is commonly known as a Shichon, Tzu Frise or Zuchon.

Size

  1. Teddy puppies are generally small but at maturity, the Shih Tzu stands between 8 and 11 inches tall at the shoulder, weighing between 9 and 16 pounds.
  2. The bichon frise on the other hand is similarly sized but ranging from 9-12 inches in height.
As a matter of fact, no matter which parent your teddy bear puppy particularly takes after, he or she will range from 8 to 12 inches in height when fully grown.
